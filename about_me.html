About My Journey

A blend of academic excellence, strategic management, and a deep passion for modern web technologies.

Academic Path

LUISS University & Research

My academic path began with a Bachelor's in Economics & Business from LUISS University in 2018, which provided the quantitative foundation for my later specialization.

In 2019, I completed a First-Level Master's in Data Analytics & Digital Marketing at LUISS Business School, graduating with a perfect 30/30. My thesis explored AI's role in Influencer Marketing through a real business case.

Building on this, I earned a Master's in Marketing from LUISS in 2021, specializing in Customer Engagement and Relationship Management, graduating with 110/110 summa cum laude 🥇. My thesis focused on the influence of social proof in e-commerce.

Passionate about the intersection of data, digital strategies, and consumer behavior, I thrive on turning insights into action.

Professional Path

From Operations to Project Management

I started my professional journey as a waiter in a pub in Rome (2015–2017) while pursuing my studies — learning resilience and high-stakes communication early on.

In 2020, I joined Procter & Gamble as a Marketing Intern, gaining hands-on experience in a global FMCG environment and applying my academic knowledge of consumer behavior directly.

In 2021, I worked as a Career Factory Consultant at LUISS, supporting students in their career development.

That same year, I joined Publicis Sapient as an intern in Digital Marketing Automation consulting. In 2022, I was promoted to Junior Project Manager, a role I still hold today, specializing in web operations for a major automotive multinational.

Tech & Growth

Evolving the Tech Stack

Over time, I've been intentionally expanding my technical toolkit to better understand and manage digital products end-to-end. What started with data analytics during my academic journey has evolved into a broader exploration of web development, automation, and AI-assisted workflows.

I've worked with tools like Python and KNIME for data analysis, and I'm progressively building hands-on experience in front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and modern web technologies. More recently, I've been integrating AI tools such as Claude into my workflow to accelerate problem-solving, content generation, and simple automation tasks.

This is not about becoming a developer, but about becoming a more effective Project Manager — one who can bridge business needs, technical constraints, and execution with greater clarity and speed.
