Learning Copilot HTML deck / Federico Landozzi

Case / 01

AI learning, organized as a product workflow.

A desktop concept that treats the AI as a tutor coordinating study over time, not as another open-ended chat box.

Problem

Learning tools often lose the thread.

Chat experiences are useful, but they can struggle to preserve study structure, progression and context across sessions.

Approach

Plan, practice, review, repeat.

The prototype frames learning as a guided loop with clear surfaces for planning, coding practice, notes and review.

RoleProduct concept and build
StackNext.js, TailwindCSS, shadcn/ui, CodeMirror
ModeAI-assisted iteration and QA

Outcome

An installable demo with a clear point of view.

The project became a demo-ready desktop app concept for Windows, useful for exploring how AI can support long-term learning workflows.
